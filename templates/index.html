How to use

  1. Open the Locket app and find your username in settings.
  2. Enter your exact username in the field above.
  3. Click Check User Info to verify the account.
  4. Review the information and click Continue to unlock Gold.
  5. Important: After activation, you must install the configuration profile below to block revocation.
  6. Restart your Locket app to see the changes.
